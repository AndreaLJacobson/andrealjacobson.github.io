Professional Experience

I am a software engineer new to the Nashville. Having lived and worked in the Seattle area for the past six years, the warmth of the south called me here to Nashville.

While in Seattle, I worked as a Project Management Consultant for Sila Solutions Group. During my time at Sila, I led many program level projects while consulting on the development of a large international airline customer service web-portal with a local original equipment manufacturer. Additionally, I provided front-end development for a subsidiary of the original equipment manufacturer. I also participated in the development of a full Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP stack database: coding, gathering requirements, and overseeing general progress of the project.

Prior to my time at Sila, I taught Mathematics for a low income high school hoping to inspire under-privileged students to pursue challenging careers in math and computer science.
